  • Simply send us a photo of your existing driveway, and tell us in 25 words or less how an Avista Resurfaced Driveway would transform your home.

Judging criteria Judging of entries will be undertaken by a panel of judges appointed by the Promoter. Judging will commence at 11th February 2016 at the offices of the Promoter. One individual entry, in the opinion of the judges, will be selected from all valid entries and judged as a winner. The Promoter’s decision is final and no correspondence will be entered into. This is a game of skill and chance plays no part in determining the winner. Each entry will be individually judged based on originality and creative merit of their entry. The judges may select additional reserve entries which they determine to be the next best, and record them in order, in the case an invalid entry or ineligible entrant is selected as the winner.
Notification The winner will be announced on 12th February 2016. The winner will be contacted by phone or email within five (5) days of the announcement. If the winner has not made contact before 19th February 2016, or in the event an invalid entry or ineligible entrant is selected as the winner, the judges will select another winner from the remaining entries which they determine to be the next best.
Publication Details The Winner will be notified by a phone call, followed by an email within 5 days of the awarded prize draw. The winner’s name will appear on the website www.avista.com.au/win for a period of 28 days from the 12th February 2016.
Prize details Major Prize One (1) Avista Resurfaced Driveway valued up to $3,000 (inc GST).
Prize Restrictions/Additional terms If any prize (or part of any prize) is unavailable, the Promoter, in its discretion, reserves the right to substitute the prize (or that part of the prize) with a prize to the equal value and/or specification.
The prize is a $3,000 driveway resurfacing project up to 60 square metres. Prizes are not transferable, saleable or exchangeable and cannot be taken or redeemed as cash or equivalent. Prize includes the cost of labour and product. The current concrete surface must also be structurally sound.
Prize Claim Date Prizes must be claimed within 1 month of the end of the Promotional Period.
Unclaimed Prize Arrangements If a Prize is not claimed by the Prize Claim Date or the entry is deemed invalid, the next best entry, as determined by the judge(s) shall be awarded the Prize.
Entry Limits The promotion is open to a maximum of one (1) entry per household.
