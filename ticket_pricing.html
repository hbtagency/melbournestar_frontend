General admission

Melbourne Star invites you to experience Melbourne in a whole new way. Day or night, you'll feel on top of the world as the Star glides on its gentle arc through the sky.

Select from a single general admission ticket or Day & Night ticket, a dual ticket providing you with the opportunity to experience the two faces of Melbourne - by day and by dusk/evening - conditions apply.

Families may select from three family ticket options with a special rate for additional children.

Each of the Melbourne Star's 21 spacious, air-conditioned cabins gives you an unparalleled, 360-degree view of the city. Walk around and take in the views from all angles, while audio commentary gives you a brief introduction to the history and significance of the sights below.
